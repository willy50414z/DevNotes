1. 產生SSL證書
	keytool -genkey -alias sias -dname "CN=Willy,OU=O1,O=CTBC,L=SungShan,ST=Taipei,C=TW" -storetype PKCS12 -keyalg RSA -keysize 2048 -keystore keystore.p12 -validity 36500
1. 透過free for ssl產生證書 (openssl用git的)
	下載憑證壓縮檔及private key
	openssl pkcs12 -export -in D:/certificate.crt -inkey D:/private.key -out keystore.p12 -name sias -CAfile D:/ca_bundle.crt
2. 將SSL證書 (keystore.p12) 放到classpath
3. 設定springboot
	server: 
	  ssl: 
		key-store: classpath:keystore.p12 #证书的路径.
		key-store-password: ENC(qA6p0q5pVQhKPTSTMX9xix7GPG7MFdTU) #证书密码，请修改为您自己证书的密码.
		keyStoreType: PKCS12  #秘钥库类型
		keyAlias: sias
